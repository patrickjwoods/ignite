Enlighten us, but make it quick.

Ignite Memphis, Vol. 8 returns November 18th at BRIDGES.

Twelve Memphians take the stage for 5-minute, passion-packed presentations. The rapid-fire bursts of ideas, stories, and energy take a unique format: 20 slides that auto-advance every 15 seconds. You'll learn, you'll laugh, and you'll definitely leave inspired.

On top of all the enlightenment, your ticket includes complimentary beer, wine, and appetizers. Remember, Vol. 7 sold out in record time, so get your tickets early.

Doors are at 7:00pm. The show starts promptly at 7:30.

Amazing talks from these equally amazing people.
